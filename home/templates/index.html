"Design is not just what it looks like. Design is how it makes you feel when nothing else is speaking."

Founded in 2018, Lumis is an independent design studio built around a single belief: that extraordinary work emerges when craft meets conviction. We partner with brands at pivotal moments — launches, pivots, reinventions — to shape how the world perceives them.

Our process is deeply collaborative, rooted in research, and relentlessly iterative. We don't chase trends. We build lasting identities that survive them.
